# SQL_sales_analytics_project
 SQL analytics project analyzing sales, customers, and products using exploratory analysis, KPI reporting, segmentation, and advanced window functions.
# Retail Sales Analytics Using SQL
This project demonstrates practical SQL analytics on a retail sales database.
It focuses on exploring the data, building reporting views, and answering
real business questions related to sales performance, customers, and products.

The project is designed to showcase job-ready SQL skills for data analyst
and BI roles.

---

## Project Scope
The analysis focuses on:
- Sales performance and KPIs
- Customer behavior and segmentation
- Product performance and profitability
- Time-based and advanced analytical insights

---

## SQL Files & Business Questions Answered

### exploratory.sql – Exploratory Data Analysis

  Performs exploratory data analysis (EDA) including data inspection,
  summary statistics, KPI calculations, and ranking analysis.

**Business questions answered:**
- What tables and columns exist in the database?
- What is the date range of sales data?
- How many:
  - total sales?
  - orders?
  - products?
  - customers?
- What is the average product price?
- Which countries, genders, and categories have the most customers?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Who are the top customers by total spending?
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- How are sold quantities distributed across countries?

---
### advanced_analytics.sql – Advanced Business Analytics

  Applies advanced SQL techniques such as window functions, running totals,
  time intelligence, performance comparisons, and contribution analysis

**Business questions answered:**
- How do sales, quantity, and customers change year over year?
- Which months perform best and worst in terms of sales?
- What are the running totals and moving averages of sales?
- How does each product perform compared to:
  - its historical average?
  - the previous year?
- Which categories contribute most to total sales?
- How are products distributed across cost ranges?
- How many customers fall into each behavioral segment?
- How does recent activity affect customer classification?
- What is the average number of orders per customer?
---

### customer_report.sql – Customer Analytics Report

 Creates a customer reporting view with lifecycle metrics, behavioral KPIs,
  and customer segmentation (VIP, Regular, New)

**Business questions answered:**
- How old are our customers and how are they distributed by age range?
- How many orders, products, and total sales does each customer generate?
- What is the customer lifespan in months?
- How recently did each customer make a purchase?
- What is the average order value per customer?
- What is the average monthly spend per customer?
- How can customers be segmented into:
  - VIP
  - Regular
  - New
based on lifespan and spending behavior?

---

### product_report.sql – Product Performance Report

  Generates a product performance report including revenue segmentation,
  lifecycle metrics, and KPIs such as average order revenue and monthly revenue

**Business questions answered:**
- How much revenue and quantity does each product generate?
- How many customers and orders does each product have?
- What is the lifespan of each product?
- When was the last time each product was sold?
- What is the average order revenue per product?
- What is the average monthly revenue per product?
- Which products are:
  - High‑Performers
  - Mid‑Range
  - Low‑Performers
based on total sales?

---
